e: mdp edges
d: dfa states
x: Augmented MDP states
 : TMDP: n^r
 : FMDP: n*r
 : MDP:  n

    - number of characters in STL expression
    - Number of small phi in Flag STL

Considered maximum
    - Each node of MDP has edges to all other nodes
